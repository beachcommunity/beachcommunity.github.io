About the Area
San Diego is a real nice place to be, especially in February. Temperatures trend 50 - 60 degrees around that time of year, so leave your puffer jackets at home. There are some excellent beaches in the area (they are also very famous, good for posting on social media). It's about 5 minutes north of the Mexican border, so the Mexican food is excellent - it is also widely considered to be the origin of the California burrito and carne asada fries. Many websites have written exhaustive lists if you care to read that kind of thing. In general, there will be lots of nice sunshine-y shenanigans for you to get up to while everyone you know back home suffers through winter.
